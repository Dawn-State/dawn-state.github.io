Dawn State Media

Social media systems built from field data, not folklore.

Dawn State Media builds data-backed distribution for apparel brands that need signal, not guesses.

The work began with a zero-to-scale Instagram experiment: a brand-new account, a proprietary data framework, and a focused attempt to understand what actually drives reach and brand awareness.

Within six months, the account was producing multiple 100k+ view reels per month and reaching over one million unique accounts per month. That dataset now informs creative testing, platform analysis, content strategy, and repeatable distribution systems.

Creative

Hook testing, product positioning, styling context, visual packaging, and reel structures designed to turn apparel into scroll-stopping creative.

Signals

Tracking retention, shares, saves, comments, timing, format, and audience response to identify which creative patterns earn distribution.

Systems

Repeatable workflows for publishing, measurement, reporting, and turning platform data into apparel brand-awareness strategy.
